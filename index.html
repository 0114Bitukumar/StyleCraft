Stylecraft has been providing furniture of original design for 70 years. Since opening our doors in
1953, we’ve grown to be a leader in commercial, residential, hospitality and education furniture.
Our seven showrooms across Australia and Singapore represent an exclusive range of European,
Asian and local furniture brands. We’re passionate about the difference that good design can
make in our daily lives.
